# NextGenResume - README

## 📄 **Introduction**

Welcome to **NextGenResume**! Our goal is to help you build a professional, ATS-friendly resume with ease. Whether you're just starting or refining your existing resume, **NextGenResume** offers the tools you need to stand out in the job market.

### Key Features:
- **ATS Compatibility Score**: Get an understanding of how well your resume will perform with Applicant Tracking Systems.
- **Keyword Gap Analysis**: Identify missing keywords for your industry to make your resume stronger.
- **Role-Specific Feedback**: Receive tailored tips to improve your resume based on the job you're aiming for.
- **Skills Gap Breakdown**: Get insights on what skills you might be missing for your target role.

## 🚀 **Core Features**

### 1. **AI-Powered Resume Builder**
   - **Themes That Shine**: Choose from four modern and professional templates—Modern, Minimal, Professional, and Creative.
   - **Smart Content Suggestions**: Get automatic content suggestions to enhance your resume.
   - **ATS-Optimized Formatting**: Build a resume that is perfectly structured to pass through ATS systems.
   - **Customizable Sections**: Edit sections like skills, achievements, and hobbies to make your resume stand out.

### 2. **AI Optimization Engine**
   - **Keyword Highlighting**: Highlight important keywords that are relevant to your job role.
   - **Content Enhancement Tips**: Improve your content with easy-to-follow tips.
   - **Industry-Specific Insights**: Get specific advice based on your industry to make your resume shine.

### 3. **Why Use NextGenResume?**
   - Real-time feedback on your resume.
   - Make your resume stand out with sleek templates and smart suggestions.
   - Maximize your chances of getting noticed by recruiters.

## 🔧 **Technologies Used**

### Frontend
- **Streamlit**: Builds an interactive and user-friendly web app to help with resume analysis.
- **HTML, CSS, JavaScript**: Used to create the structure, style, and interactivity of the web pages.

### Backend
- **Streamlit**: Also manages backend processes and integrates machine learning models for the analysis.
- **Python**: The programming language used to implement all the features and logic.

### Database
- **SQLite3**: Stores and retrieves resume data for processing and analysis.

### Modules
- **spaCy**: Helps analyze resumes for ATS compatibility and keyword gaps.
- **Python-docx**: Allows editing of Word documents for resume customizations.
- **PyPDF2**: Helps process and analyze resumes in PDF format.
- **scikit-learn**: Powers machine learning models to optimize resumes.
- **Plotly**: Creates charts to visualize skills gaps and keyword analysis.
- **NLTK**: Used for processing and analyzing text data in resumes.
- **openpyxl**: Reads and writes Excel files to handle data export and visualization.

## ⚙️ **How It Works**

### Step 1: Upload or Create a Resume
   - Upload your resume in **PDF** or **Word** format, or create a brand new one using our AI-powered builder.

### Step 2: Analyze Your Resume
   - **ATS Compatibility**: Check how well your resume is optimized for ATS systems.
   - **Keyword Insights**: Identify and fill keyword gaps relevant to your target job.
   - **Skills Gap Analysis**: See which key skills you're missing for the role you're applying for.

### Step 3: Build a Stunning Resume
   - Choose from **4 unique templates** and customize sections to match your skills, achievements, and career path.

### Step 4: Download & Apply
   - Export your final resume as a **PDF** and apply for jobs with confidence.
